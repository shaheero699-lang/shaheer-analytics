# Veltrix RevOps Intelligence

Enterprise Revenue Operations Intelligence System designed for executive decision-making, operational visibility, forecasting, customer intelligence, and business performance optimization.

---

## Core Objectives

This platform is built to answer:

- What happened?
- What is happening now?
- What is likely to happen next?

Using:

- SQL analytics
- Python data workflows
- forecasting models
- Power BI dashboards
- business intelligence systems

---

## Business Domains

The project simulates a real-world B2B SaaS ecosystem including:

- customers
- subscriptions
- revenue
- churn
- sales pipeline
- customer support
- marketing attribution
- customer health scoring
