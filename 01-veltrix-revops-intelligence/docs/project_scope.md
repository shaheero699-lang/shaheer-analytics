# Project Scope — Enterprise Revenue Operations Intelligence Platform

## Company Overview

Veltrix AI is a fictional mid-market B2B SaaS company that provides AI-powered revenue operations and customer intelligence software for growing businesses.

The platform helps organizations manage:

- sales pipelines
- customer onboarding
- support operations
- customer retention
- customer health monitoring
- revenue forecasting
- executive KPI tracking

Veltrix operates on a recurring subscription revenue model and serves SaaS companies, agencies, e-commerce businesses, and mid-market organizations.

---

# Business Problem

After a period of rapid growth, Veltrix AI began experiencing operational and revenue challenges including:

- increasing customer churn
- declining retention
- inconsistent sales performance
- onboarding inefficiencies
- support SLA breaches
- rising customer acquisition costs
- weak forecasting accuracy
- declining expansion revenue
- poor executive visibility across departments

Leadership lacked a centralized intelligence system to identify:

- revenue leakage
- customer churn risk
- operational inefficiencies
- high-value customer segments
- profitable acquisition channels
- retention bottlenecks

---

# Project Objective

The objective of this project is to build a centralized Enterprise Revenue Operations Intelligence Platform that provides:

## Historical Analytics

Understanding historical business performance through:

- revenue analysis
- churn trends
- cohort retention analysis
- funnel conversion analysis
- support escalation analysis
- regional performance tracking

---

## Operational Intelligence

Monitoring current operational performance including:

- live KPI tracking
- customer health monitoring
- support SLA performance
- onboarding progress
- sales pipeline visibility
- marketing performance

---

## Predictive Intelligence

Forecasting future business outcomes through:

- revenue forecasting
- churn prediction
- customer risk scoring
- retention probability analysis
- ARR and MRR forecasting
- growth projections

---

# Departments Included

This platform integrates operational and analytical data from:

- Sales
